# Position Brief
*[Date] — [Idea or proposal label]*

## The claim
[One or two sentences. What you're proposing and why it matters. Specific enough that a skeptic knows what they're evaluating.]

## The logic
[The reasoning behind the claim. What evidence or experience supports it. What assumptions it rests on.]

## Objections anticipated
[The strongest case against this. What a skeptical stakeholder, a peer who's been burned, or a resource-constrained leader would push back on. Be honest — a brief that ignores real objections isn't ready.]

## What's needed to move
[Who needs to say yes. What resource, access, or decision is required. What the first concrete step is.]

## What happens if we don't
[The cost of inaction or delay. Optional — include only if it genuinely strengthens the case.]
